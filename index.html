Recipes

  • Chocolate Chip Cookies

    • Ingredients

    • Greek Yoghurt
    • Vanilla Extract
    • 1 stick of butter
    • Flour
    • Backing Soda
    • Salt
    • Chocolate Chips

    Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 375 Celsius.
    2. Mix yoghurt, butter, and vanilla extract.
    3. In a separate bowl,mix the flour, baking soda, and salt together.
    4. Combine the dry and wet ingredients together.
    5. Add the chocolate chips
